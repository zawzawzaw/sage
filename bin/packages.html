  • Cottesloe Beach

    Views this famous beach on the way to Fremantle

    Explore this historical port situated on the mouth of the Swan River and find bargains at the markets (Friday, Saturday and Sunday only)

  • Swan River Cruise

    Cruise back along the Swan River from Fremantle

Rottnest Island Day Tour including Guided Bus Tour and Lunch

  • Explore the sights of Rottnest Island in air-conditioned comfort

  • Take in the historical, cultural and natural interest points of Rottnest Island

  • Lunch at Rottnest Lodge

  • View the annual migration of Humpback Whales from September to November

  • Enjoy the array of wildlife that inhabits Rottnest

  • The vast natural playground has over 63 beaches and 20 bays

  • Rottnest Island has a large range of activities to keep the family entertained for hours

  • Splash and slide on the floating aqua park in Thomson Bay located just a short walking distance from the main settlement is the family fun park

  • Rottnest boasts an incredibly rich heritage. Take a tour up to Oliver Hill on the

  • Captain Hussey, view relics at the Rottnest Museum, Lomas Cottage and Pilot Boat House

Highlights

Pinnacles Desert with 4WD Adventure, Koalas and Kangaroos

  • Guildford

    History and charm on the Perth outskirts

  • The Swan Valley

    Colonial towns, crafts and Western Australia's first wine growing region

  • Caversham Wildlife Park

    An opportunity to see koalas, wombat and kangaroo close up

  • Look for native wildflowers from September through to November

  • Cross vast big sky scrub and pasturelands

  • Look for kangaroos and emus on the endless plains

  • Visit a lobster farm on a guided tour

  • Enjoy a picnic lunch at the coastal town of Cervantes

  • See Namburg National Park - home of the Pinnacles

  • Take a guided walk through the Pinnacles desert and it's limestone pillars created by the forces of nature

  • Take a break at the cray fishing port of Lancelin on the Indian Ocean with time for a short stroll on the beach

  • Enjoy thrills with giant four wheel driving across and down the massive Lancelin sand dunes - exhilarating!

  • Try your hand at sandboarding on the dunes - a really fun experience for any age

Highlights

Swan Valley Fruit Orchard and Wildlife Park Tour

  • Drive past the sights of the Swan River, The Bell Tower and the Perth City skyline on your way to the Swan Valley

  • Have a unique wildlife experience at Caversham Park where you’ll meet native Australian animals and birds including Kangaroos, Koalas, wombats, local farm and poultry animals.

  • Get a taste of farm life at Molly’s Farm, meet reptiles, birds of prey.

  • Visit a local winery to enjoy delicious tastings of local wine and other delicious produce, including chocolate from the famous local Chocolate Company.

  • Explore and be involved in the colourful rich heritage of the Aboriginal culture, arts and wooden crafts, visit the Maalinup Aboriginal Gallery for the insights of the indigenous cultural activities, talks, performances and bush tucker, as well as a gallery full of local Aboriginal art, exquisitely different hand painted gifts and high quality Australian made souvenirs.

  • Drive into the Perth Hills to a local orchard and take the opportunity to purchase fresh or/and dried, seasonal produced fruits like Nectarines, Peaches, Plums, Cherries, Apricots, Persimmons, Apples, Avocados, Pears, Oranges and Mandarins and also have them box packed to take back home.

  • Choose to be dropped off to the doorstep of your accommodation or, alternatively, Northbridge, Chinatown or Crown Casino for dinner (at own cost).
